- WHAT
    The DISCOUNT column on the WORK_ORDERS table has been deprecated and will be replaced by the new DISCOUNTVALUE column.
- WHY
    There was a precision error in the discount calculation in percentage values, so we changed to a calculation in monetary values.
- WHAT
    The roundflag column in the WORK_ORDERS table has been deprecated and will no longer be filled in.
- WHY
    With the new DISCOUNTVALUE column for calculating discounts, it no longer makes sense to keep the ROUNDFLAG column.
